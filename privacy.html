Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Introduction

Speed Reading (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application on iOS.

Information we collect

Information you provide

If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address or other details you choose to send. We use that information only to respond to your request.

Usage and diagnostics

We may use analytics or crash reporting tools to understand how the app is used and to fix bugs. Such tools may collect device identifiers, app version, and event data in aggregated or pseudonymous form, as permitted by Apple’s policies and your device settings (including App Tracking Transparency where applicable).

Local app data

Reading progress, preferences, and similar data may be stored on your device to provide core functionality. Deleting the app typically removes locally stored data unless otherwise noted in the app.

Purchases

Subscription and in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple may provide us limited transaction-related information needed to validate entitlements.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app
  • Understand feature usage and reliability
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist us (e.g., analytics or hosting) subject to confidentiality obligations, or when required by law, to protect rights, or in connection with a business transfer.

Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required by law.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your choices

You can control certain data collection through iOS settings (for example, privacy and tracking preferences). You may also contact us to ask about access, correction, or deletion where applicable by law.

International users

If you access the app from outside the country where we operate, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and revise the “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at privacy@speedreading.app or visit https://speedreading.app.
